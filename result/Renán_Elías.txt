Renán Elías

Renán Elías (February 2, 1915 – 1941) was a Peruvian aviator who fought during the conflict between Peru and Ecuador in 1941.
His studies were conducted at the National College Centenary "San Luis Gonzaga" of Ica.Joined the Aviation Corps of Peru on March 9, 1935 at only twenty years of age.
