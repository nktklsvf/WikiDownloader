Josef Allram

Josef Allram (Schrems, November 22, 1860- Mödling, December 29, 1941) was an Austrian writer and teacher.
He studied Education Sciences.

Works
	Waldviertler Geschichten, 1900
	Der 1000. Patient, 1903
	Hamerling und seine Heimat, 1905
	Der letzte Trieb, 1911
