Erling Broch

Erling Broch (22 February 1875 – 4 September 1941) was a Norwegian judge.
He was born in Horten to brewery owner Johan Anthony Zincke Broch and Fanny Caroline Harriet Gamborg.[1] He was a brother of Hjalmar Broch,[2] Olaf Broch,[3] Lagertha Broch[4] and Nanna Broch, father of Lisbeth Broch, and father-in-law of Vilhelm Evang.[5]
He graduated as cand.jur. in 1899, and was named as a Supreme Court Justice from 1924.[1]
