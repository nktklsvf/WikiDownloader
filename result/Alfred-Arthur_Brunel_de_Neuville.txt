Alfred-Arthur Brunel de Neuville

Alfred-Arthur Brunel de Neuville (1852–1941) was a French painter.
Brunel de Neuville died in 1941 and is buried in Montmartre Cemetery in Paris. His works can be seen in museums at Béziers, Brest, Chateau Thierry, and Louviers.
