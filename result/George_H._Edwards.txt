George H. Edwards

George H. Edwards (May 25, 1860 - December 28, 1941) was Mayor of Kansas City, Missouri from 1916 to 1917.
He was born in St. Louis, Missouri and moved to Kansas City in 1888,[1] first living at 609 Brooklyn Avenue and then 3533 Harrison Boulevard while mayor.[2]
He was the secretary and manager of Edwards & Sloane Jewelry.[3]
