Bengt Djurberg

Bengt Djurberg (23 July 1898 – 2 November 1941) was a Swedish actor and singer.[1] He appeared in about 25 roles in films 1919 and 1940. His film debut was in Mauritz Stiller's film Sången om den eldröda blomman in 1919.
