Ugo de Carolis

Ugo de Carolis (born 1887 and killed in action 12 December 1941) was a Brigadier-General and commander of the 52 Motorised Division Torino on the eastern front during World War II. He was a recipient of the Knight's Cross of the Iron Cross.

Awards
	Medaglia d'oro al valor militare
	Knight's Cross of the Iron Cross on 9 February 1942 as Generale di brigada and commander of the Italian 52 Motorised Division Torino[1]
