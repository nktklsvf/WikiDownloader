Teddy Barton

Edward Victor "Teddy" Barton (1 January 1904 – 13 November 1941) was an English footballer who played as a right half for Everton and Tranmere Rovers.[4][5]
He was most remember for his long career at Tranmere and . He made 260 appearances for the club, scoring 5 goals.[6][4]
Barton later lived in Prenton, Cheshire, and was survived by his wife, Alice Elizabeth.[7] He died in 1941 at only 37 years old, after suffering from an illness for several months.[6]
