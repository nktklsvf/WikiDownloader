Hélène Edlund

Hélène Sofie Edlund (20 October 1858 – 30 June 1941) was a Swedish photographer.[1]
In the 1890s Edlund collaborated with Artur Hazelius documenting the buildings and nature of Skansen.[1]
Her nineteenth century photographs of folk costume are used to decorate locker doors in the Nordic Museum.[2]
